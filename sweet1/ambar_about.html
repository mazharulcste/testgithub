About Company & Owners

Ambar Garden Furniture was incorporated under the laws of Spain in 2000 and is owned and operated by two partners, Ana Elena Gastaldi and Eduardo Hualde. Both partners are equally involved in its operations and management and have extensive knowledge and experience in the household and commercial Garden Furniture industry for the past 25 years.

Ambar Garden Furniture is located along Umm Suqeim Rd. Ambar Garden Furniture strives to maintain its owners’ principals of unmatched design fused with high quality and service while offering a wide range of styles and product categories to suit all of our customer’s needs and tastes. Our furniture is made from “eco-Green” fiber and top quality Aluminum.

Vision & Mission

To offer our customers quality garden furniture with unique designs.

- Ana Gastaldi
